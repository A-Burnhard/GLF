Become a VOLUNTEER Today

Global Leaders Foundation is looking for people with the following skills and attributes

Passionate about making a difference in the world

Ability to adapt to different environment

Ability to take initiative and direction

Well-organized

Flexible and open-minded

Ability to interact respectfully with people from diverse racial, cultural, and economic backgrounds

Team player

Innovative/Creative

Volunteer Placement Procedures include:

1. Selection of programme type, duration, and project portfolio of interest. You provide details on skills, interests, academic background, and professional experiences.

2. Application evaluation and project preferences by programme director to assign applicant to the most appropriate project

3. Depending upon your length of stay, the programme Director will send you the cost for the following

Airport Pick-up

Accommodation (private room in a comfortable home-stay setting)

Three meals daily

24/7 In-country support

Accra city tour upon arrival

Volunteer placement arrangements and follow-up

Personalized Souvenir

Visitation to a partner orphanage home

Airport Drop-off

Volunteers who are staying for longer periods of time and have higher levels of project related experience or language skills are more likely to receive their first project preference.

Responsibilities include: Helping to coordinate projects and events, preparing materials, research, assisting staff with event production, answering phone calls, maintaining database, event production and visitation to placement sites.

Benefits include a chance to help make a difference, be part of a progressive community and exposure to relevant issues.
